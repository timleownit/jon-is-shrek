## The Legend of Jonathan Chiam Heng Wei, the NUS IT Malaysian Contract Engineer

Once upon a time, in the land of NUS IT, there lived a legendary Malaysian Contract Engineer named Jonathan Chiam Heng Wei. One fateful day, as he was coding late into the night, Jonathan wrote the magical line:

```
shrek.instantiate()
```

And thus, a transformation began! From that moment onward, Jonathan didn’t just embody the wisdom, resilience, and gentle heart of Shrek—he became Shrek himself. Green, bold, misunderstood, but always kind beneath the layers, Jonathan’s story reminds us that true greatness comes from within (and sometimes in the form of unexpected code).

Welcome to his swamp!
