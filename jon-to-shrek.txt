How to Become Shrek if Your Name is Jonathan:

1. Embrace your inner ogre. Be unapologetically yourself, no matter what others think.
2. Spend time in nature. Swamps are a plus, but any outdoor time counts!
3. Master the art of the Scottish accent—optional, but highly recommended.
4. Befriend a talkative sidekick (preferably named Donkey).
5. Protect your privacy, but open your heart to new friendships.
6. Enjoy onion layers: metaphorical and real.
7. Unlock transformation by writing code: shrek.instantiate() in any programming language of your choice.
8. Celebrate your green energy!
